Frequently asked questions

Is this REALLY legal?

Our lawyer doesn’t mess around. She wouldn’t let us do it if it wasn’t. In every state Sotipsy operates in, we thoroughly go through the proper legal process. We’ve done it so many times, it feels like we’ve all earned law degrees by now.

How long does it take to get started?

We’ve done it in as little as 48 hours before and since we’re pretty competitive, we’re always trying to beat that record. Generally, you can expect that it will take around 5-7 days, but it depends on a variety of factors. We can give you a better idea once we start talking.

Does my store need special permits or licenses to deliver alcohol?

You might. We strongly encourage you to contact regulatory bodies in your state to ensure you’re properly certified to deliver alcohol to customers.

Who does the deliveries?

Stores and their staff are responsible for packing and delivering all orders received through Sotipsy. For more information on external delivery solutions in your area, contact us.

Who determines my delivery zone, hours, fees, minimums, etc.?

You do. We’ve got a pretty good idea what factors will set you up for success and will happily help you figure out what’s best for your store. And nothing is set in stone. You’re always able to adjust based on demand, capability and desire.

Can I be the exclusive Sotipsy store for my area?

We operate on a marketplace model and our platform is open to any qualified store. We will never guarantee exclusivity to any one store in a given area.
