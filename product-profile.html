Expert Notes

Espolòn Reposado boasts a brilliant golden hue thanks to its painstaking production process. It is aged for three to five months in lightly charred, new American oak barrels until it achieves a bold character with a long, spicy finish with a mild wood flavor. On the nose, you'll notice an unmistakable spiciness with hints of caramel fudge, which pairs seamlessly with the tequila's bold, round palate. Flavor-wise, Espolòn Reposado is medium-to-full-bodied with vanilla, sweet tropical fruit and brown spices. Thanks to the brand's impeccable tequila-making process, Espolòn Reposado has earned many awards over the years, including the Gold Medal at the 2011 San Francisco World Spirits Award. Sip it straight or use it to create your favorite cocktails. One of our favorites is the Mexican Mule, which features Espolòn Reposado with ginger beer and lime juice.
