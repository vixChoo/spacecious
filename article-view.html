Sippin' In The Rain

You know what they say: April showers bring May flowers, but April wine is simply divine. Okay, we added that second part, but the idiom still rings true. Whether you love or hate the rain, the weather is never too poor to enjoy a glass of wine. Here are 5 of our all-time favorite springtime wines, because when it rains, we pour.

Whitehaven Sauvignon Blanc.

When the rain has got you down, just picture yourself in the lush, green New Zealand vineyards that produced this medium-bodied, finely-balanced Sauv Blanc. Aromas of blackcurrant leaf and jalapeño mingle with notes of grapefruit, guava and passionfruit for a long, clean finish.
